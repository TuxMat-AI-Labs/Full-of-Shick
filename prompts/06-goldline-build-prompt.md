# Build prompt: The Gold Line (black-and-gold concept)

This is the prompt and concept that produced `goldline/`. The ask built
on the dossier ("I love the animations and scrolling details"); the
concept block records the direction that answered it. TuxMat guardrails
from 03/04 apply throughout.

---

## The ask

Give me another unexpected, nothing-overlooked design option. This time
ensure an overall black look throughout. Bold areas of our brand gold,
too. (Following the dossier: "I love the animations and scrolling
details.")

## The concept that answered it

THE THROUGH-LINE
- Promote the dossier's draw-on-scroll from a flourish to the entire
  organizing principle. One unbroken gold line of light (the laser's
  path) travels the whole page from hero to order and never breaks.
- Between panels it runs as a literal vertical gold thread connector.
  Inside each panel it becomes a single-stroke SVG figure that draws
  itself on scroll.

THE SEGMENTS
- Segment 01: the line draws the mat's contour (outer raised edge,
  inner floor line).
- Segment 02: the line rasters back and forth, the path of the scan.
- Segment 03: the line loops three times, one per TriForce™ layer.
- Segment 04: coverage tiers as cards; the selected tier turns solid
  gold.
- Segment 05: the order. "The line ends at your door."

BLACK WITH BOLD GOLD
- Pure black (#0a0a0b) throughout. The boldest beats invert to solid
  brand-gold panels with black type: a full-bleed "Because You Notice."
  statement, and a gold proof panel where 4.8★ and up-to-2.5x count up
  on entry (footnoted in place). Floating add-to-order strip is solid
  gold; order drawer is black with a gold spine; checkout success draws
  a gold checkmark with the same stroke animation as the line.

TYPE
- Space Grotesk (uppercase display statements), IBM Plex Mono (labels,
  segment numbers, prices), Inter (body). Gold reserved as the single
  accent against black, used boldly in the inversions.

THE PRACTICAL SUPERPOWER
- Like the dossier: zero photography dependence. Every figure is inline
  SVG line art. Shippable without a single asset delivery.

GUARDRAILS (same as 03/04)
- Approved claims only, footnoted in place (2.5x Tesla Model Y
  comparison; 4.8★ Google Ratings; "Limited Lifetime Warranty" exact).
  No em-dashes, no exclamation marks, sentence case. Demo checkout
  labeled "demo · no payment is processed." Sample TIERS and VEHICLES
  data marked for live Shopify replacement. Vanilla HTML/CSS/JS plus
  GSAP CDN, mobile-first, no horizontal overflow, full reduced-motion
  fallback (line renders complete, counters show final values).
