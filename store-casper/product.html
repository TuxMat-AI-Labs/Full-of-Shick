Questions

Before you order.

Will these fit my exact 2026 Audi A3? +
Yes. Each set is developed using 3D laser scanning for a specific year, make, and model. Use the vehicle selector above to confirm your fit.
Are the mats waterproof? +
The mats are water-resistant. The surface and raised walls channel liquid away from the carpet, and the surface wipes clean easily.
What does the warranty cover? +
Every set is backed by our Limited Lifetime Warranty, alongside a 30-day money-back guarantee.
How is this different from conventional mats? +
Conventional mats are a universal shape trimmed by hand and cover the floor only. TuxMat is laser-scanned to your exact vehicle, with up to 2.5x more coverage* and TriForce™ multi-layer construction.
